What is Energy Portal?

Energy Portal provides an educational service to citizens, policy makers, and organizations who are interested in energy consumption patterns in the United States. We designed a model built with robust machine learning techniques that will show you how your energy consumption compares to others and how your community fits into the national patterns of energy consumption.

How to use Energy Portal?

Use Energy Portal in 3 easy steps.
  1. Fill out the Energy Form below.
  2. Click on submit.
  3. Review your report page and see how you can improve your energy consumption footprint.
