Energy Portal

How energy efficient are you?

Individual Report

This section gives the users a grade from A+ to D comparing their energy consumption to other individuals in their geographical area. It also provides additional information about their energy consumption patterns through interactive graphs.

Community Report

The community section of the report provides a community grade from A+ to D comparing the energy consumption of their community to other communities in the US. This section also provides more information through interactive histograms and maps.

Forecast

The final part of the report provides the users with a forecast of how their energy consumption will change across time. Here we provide the users with a sensitivity analysis, that enables users to change different factors and behaviours and see their effect on energy consumption.

Method

The algorithms and data used to create the report.
